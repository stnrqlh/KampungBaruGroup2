Social Media

Facebook Community

The residents of Kampung Baru have a Facebook community that is used by the residents in the area. They use this Facebook to share news about what is happening in Kampung Baru. In addition, they also use it to promote the products and food sold by the residents of Kampung Baru. Only Kampung Baru residents are allowed to post on Facebook.

Organization (PKB)

Facebook

This PKB facebook page shares information based on PKB and also information related to Kampung Baru. This Facebook is also used to make life sharing that is common to the residents of Kampung Baru.

Instagram

Their Instagram posts about activities carried out with the villagers and also programs with DBKL. This Instagram also promotes Kampong Bharu to further improve the progress of local residents there.

Website

Their website has an objective and an organizational chart of the Kampung Baru Corporation. They also provide space and forms for residents to voice their opinions. They also have a form to give feedback about Kampung Baru to outsiders to improve on the shortcomings they provide.
